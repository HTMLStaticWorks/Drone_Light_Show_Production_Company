How We Build Your Sky Story

Our digital animation pipeline translates standard 3D files (FBX, OBJ) directly into synchronized flight trajectory coordinate pathways. Every single drone behaves like a physical pixel in the night sky, producing high-resolution graphics.

Creative Phase Steps

2D Storyboarding

Establishing keyframes, text displays, and color theme shifts corresponding to audio tracks.

3D Rigging & Modeling

Optimizing wireframes to maintain visibility at varying flight angles and viewer distances.

Path & Collision Checks

Simulating paths using automated collision algorithms to verify physical separations.

Music Sync

Aligning audio playback boards with flight timelines down to millisecond accuracy.
