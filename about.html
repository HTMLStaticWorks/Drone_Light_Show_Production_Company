Our Mission & Tech Pioneers

Blending advanced aeronautics and 3D computer animation to craft beautiful light displays across the world.

Innovation in Every Pixel of the Sky

Founded by aerospace engineers and computational artists, AeroGlow has grown to become a premium supplier of large-scale drone performances. We coordinate flight lines and colors down to the millisecond, turning skies into digital display canvases.

With offices in three continents, we coordinate global logistics, custom hardware fabrication, airspace clearance, and storyboard choreography in-house.

Key Milestones

2020 Launched first 100-drone synchronized show.
2022 Approved for nationwide FAA Part 107 night waivers.
2024 Successfully completed a 1,000-drone stadium display.
Active 500+ successful operations completed worldwide.
