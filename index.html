Our Production Blueprint

From first concept sketch to the final automated sync, here is how we safely and successfully bring your vision into the heavens.

01. Discovery

We discuss event objectives, site geometry, budget constraints, and regulatory requirements.

02. Creative Design

Our 3D animators storyboard flight formations and create color palette timelines.

03. Simulation

Verify collisions and safety tolerances using specialized computer emulation routines.

04. Deployment

On-site setup, coordinate calibration, RTK GPS checks, and localized ground inspections.

05. Live Performance

Launch the show. Gaze upwards as the fleet executes the choreographed flight plan.
